1. Privacy Policy

EasyPay Nigeria ("we", "our", "us") operates the EasyPay mobile application.

1.1 Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when you register:

  • Personal Information: Full Name, Phone Number, Email Address, Bank Details
  • Transaction Information: Investment amount, profit history, withdrawals
  • Device Information: Device model, IP address for security

1.2 How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  1. Create and manage your EasyPay account
  2. Process investments and profit payouts
  3. Send updates, notifications, and support
  4. Improve app security and prevent fraud

1.3 Data Protection

Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

1.4 Data Deletion

2. Risk Disclaimer & Terms

IMPORTANT RISK DISCLOSURE

2.1 Not a Bank

EasyPay Nigeria is NOT a bank and is NOT regulated by the Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN).

2.2 Investment Risk

All investments involve risk. Profits are not guaranteed. Past performance does not guarantee future results.

2.3 Lifetime Model

EasyPay is designed as a lifetime investment. It is NOT a quick get-rich scheme or short-term investment.

2.4 Withdrawals

Only your profit is withdrawable anytime. Your capital remains active in your investment plan.

2.5 Not a Ponzi Scheme

EasyPay operates on a sustainable reinvestment model. No new user funds are used to pay old users.

2.6 Agreement

By using EasyPay, you agree that you understand these risks and are investing at your own discretion.
